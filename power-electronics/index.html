Suggested learning sequence

  1. Power factor: understand why the grid cares about both phase displacement and harmonic distortion.
  2. Boost PFC: see why placing the inductor at the input gives the controller a smooth current to shape.
  3. CrCM vs CCM: compare simple valley-switched control against fixed-frequency average current mode.
  4. DC bus: understand why the PFC output is usually above the highest rectified AC peak.
  5. LLC converter: use resonance and magnetizing current to reduce switching loss in the downstream isolated stage.
